About TheLuxar

TheLuxar is a premium brand known for its finely crafted jewelry, luxury watches, and refined perfumes, created for clients with a taste for elegance.

African craftsmanship meets modern elegance to create exceptional accessories that celebrate your unique style.

Our Story

Founded by Jospen Wolongwo in Douala, Cameroon, TheLuxar began with a vision to bring premium quality jewelry, watches, and fine fragrances to the African luxury market. Drawing inspiration from both traditional African craftsmanship and contemporary global design trends, we've created a distinctive brand that celebrates elegance with an authentic African perspective.

Inspired by Cameroon's rich cultural heritage and artistic traditions, Jospen assembled a team of skilled artisans to create collections that blend traditional African motifs with contemporary designs, using ethically sourced materials that honor both people and planet.

Today, TheLuxar stands as a leading name in luxury jewelry in Cameroon, with creations that celebrate African elegance while offering affordable options that respect the economic realities of our local market. Based in Makepe, Douala, we proudly serve customers throughout Cameroon and ship worldwide.

Mission & Values

Our Mission

TheLuxar's mission is to redefine luxury in Cameroon and Africa by offering high-quality jewelry and timepieces that honor African heritage while remaining accessible to our community. We aim to elevate the confidence and style of our customers through exceptional accessories that represent both cultural significance and modern elegance, crafted with attention to detail and offered at fair prices.

Our Values

  • Authenticity: We create pieces that genuinely reflect African heritage and culture while meeting international quality standards.
  • Excellence: We pursue the highest standards of craftsmanship and customer service in every aspect of our business.
  • Accessibility: We believe luxury should be attainable, offering quality pieces at price points that respect the economic realities of our market.
  • Sustainability: We prioritize ethical sourcing, environmentally responsible practices, and supporting local artisans and communities.
  • Transparency: We believe in clear and honest communication about our materials, craftsmanship, and ethical practices.
